WeShare Movement

Let us acknowledge that we live in challenging times. Declining health, global inequality, and climate change are just a few of the challenges we face today. Core to the problem is increasing disconnection from our bodies, one another, and our Earth.

In truth, the cure is within us. We create a more beautiful, loving world for us and our children every time we choose love and connection over separation. We believe that sharing freely within our local communities is one such choice. With each item, service, or experience we share, we tap into a collective abundance that enables all of us to thrive.

We invite you to choose love. Join us in our movement to bring people together through sharing.

Technology That Connects

We believe in a world where everyone has access to everything they need to thrive. To be this change, we're creating technology to facilitate sharing within and between local communities. We aspire to create online and offline spaces where we can all:

  1. Give what we can and get what we need.
  2. Be in community that has our back.
  3. Contribute to a sustainable world.
